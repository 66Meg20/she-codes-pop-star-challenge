Gaku Yaotome

Gaku Yaotome (八乙女楽やおとめがく) is rough-spoken and has a fairly intimidating aura about him, so there are a lot of rumors swirling around about how nasty the president of Yaotome Pro’s son must be. For his fans, he’s the sexy guy they want to embrace them the most.

Tenn Kujo

Tenn Kujo (九条天くじょうてん), the people’s angel who thinks only of his fans. His sweet, innocent demeanor on stage and perfection in just about everything he does endears him to his fans, and Tenn’s existence itself was considered something of a “secret weapon” during TRIGGER’s formation and early days. Tenn was even called by fans and co-workers "Embodiment of Perfection".

“ Even when the whole world is despairing, it is our job to sing, to smile. ” —Tenn Kujo

Ryunosuke Tsunashi

Ryunosuke Tsunashi (十龍之介つなしりゅのすけ), is actually a very shy and timid soul, who has a difficult time talking with women due to growing up with his brothers. There's also a good argument for Ryunosuke's naivete, if his comments to the woman at his father's hotel are any indication.

“ If you come to understand each other, bit by bit… You’ll become the best team. We were like that too. ” —Ryunosuke Tsunashi
